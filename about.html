About Us

Twelve years of early mornings, flour-dusted aprons, and baking with heart.

Our Story

Started small. Still baking the same way.

Sweet Crumbs Bakery opened its doors in 2012 with one oven, two bakers, and a firm belief that good bread should never be rushed. What began as a passion project quickly became a fixture in the local community.

We haven't changed the recipes. We haven't outsourced the baking. Every loaf, every pastry, and every cake is still made by hand in our Edinburgh kitchen the same way it was on day one.

2012

Sweet Crumbs opens on Baker Street with a sourdough loaf and a dream.

2015

Pastry range introduced. The croissant becomes an instant bestseller.

2018

Custom cake orders launched after customers kept asking for celebrations cakes.

2024

Seating added inside the shop. People stay longer. That feels right.

What we stand for

Three principles that guide everything we do.

🌾

Freshness

Nothing leaves our kitchen more than a few hours old. We bake through the night so your morning starts with something genuinely fresh.

🤝

Community

We source locally where we can, we know our regulars by name, and we sponsor the local school bake sale every year. This bakery belongs to the neighbourhood.

⭐

Quality

We use real butter, fresh eggs, and flour from a Scottish mill. No shortcuts. If we wouldn't eat it ourselves, it doesn't go on the shelf.
