Our Story

Baked with purpose, served with love.

Sweet Crumbs started as a one-oven operation in a small Edinburgh kitchen. Over twelve years it has grown into a neighbourhood institution but the recipes haven't changed a bit.

  • Locally sourced flour and dairy
  • No artificial preservatives or flavourings
  • Everything baked fresh from 4am daily
  • Custom orders welcome with 48h notice
