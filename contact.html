Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance do I need to order a custom cake?

We ask for at least 48 hours' notice. For large or elaborate designs, a week ahead is better. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

Do you deliver?

We currently don't offer delivery, but click-and-collect is available. Order by phone or email and pick up at the shop at your chosen time.

Do you cater for allergies or dietary requirements?

We can accommodate some requirements with advance notice. Please speak to us before ordering we cannot guarantee allergen-free products.

Do you do corporate or event orders?

Yes we regularly supply offices, events, and school functions. Get in touch with your numbers and requirements and we'll put together a quote.
